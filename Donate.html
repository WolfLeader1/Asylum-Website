Donation Rewards

When you donate to the Asylum, you'll get awesome rewards in-game! Donations are cumulative, meaning if you donate $10 one day and $15 another, you'll receive the $25 rewards. They add up so that you can build on rewards over time.
Hover over and click the donate items to see what you can get!

  • Ability to purchase exclusive donor t-shirts in clothing stores.
  • Ability to purchase the following vehicle skins:
    • Orange Speedboat
    • Purple Offroad
    • Red Jetski
    • Orange Jeep
    • Olive Jeep
  • 5 additional forum likes per day (30 total).
